\section{Lesson 7}
\begin{enumerate}
    \item The inverse matrix of a square matrix, if it exists, is unique.
    \item The inverse of a product is the product of the inverse taken in the opposite order.
    \item The inverse of a transpose is the transpose of the inverse.
    \item Linear systems with an invertible coefficient matrix have unique solutions.
    \item Inverse linear transformations correspond with inverse matrices.
\end{enumerate}
